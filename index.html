Web of Lies

"We are all innocent, the question is: Do you believe it?"

When a large New York City company is burned down with the boss inside of it, it's only a matter of time before someone starts to look into it. The suspects, Katherine, Imogen, and Lydia, all have different sotries. Claiming they didn't do it, the girls dive into a conspiracy that shatters their lives and makes it almost impossible to pick up the pieces. Told by suspects, police reports, witnesses, and more, nothing adds up when the pieces are put together. everyone has a different story, and when everyone is lying, how do you find the truth?

About the Author

Addisyn Budnick is a thirteen year old girl who loves to read and write. Currently Web of Lies is her only book, though she plans to write more in the future. Web of Lies can be found on Amazon, where she self-publishes her work. Addisyn plays basketball and swims for her high school team and hopes to make it into Harvard to study engineering or forensics.(She can't decide which) She's written since she was little and hopes to be able to publish more of her work as she writes it.

The book, cover art, and website were all created by Addisyn Budnick.
